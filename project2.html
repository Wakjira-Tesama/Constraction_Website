AREKA TOWN WATER SUPPLY PROJECT

The project includes construction of road crossings, sewerage lines, guardhouses, public latrines, and 29 anchor blocks, along with 15 water points and a dome-shaped walkway shade. It involves the supply and installation of DCI and HDPE pipes and fittings, a 100m³ service reservoir, a 50m³ collection chamber, and electromechanical systems. Additional works include landscape development and a recreation center, ensuring a complete and functional utility infrastructure.
