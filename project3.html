Gimbi Site Center for Handicapped and OVC Children treatment project

The project includes the supply and installation of 13km HDPE pipelines, construction of a 100m³ service reservoir, 100m³ collection chamber, and spring development, along with sewerage lines and road crossings. It also covers construction of office and workshop buildings, public and communal latrines, guardhouses, a dome-shaped walkway shade, and internal reinforced concrete roads. Electromechanical installation, 29 anchor blocks, and full utility infrastructure ensure complete system functionality and readiness.
