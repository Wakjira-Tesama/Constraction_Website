Dalacho Water supply project

This project involves the supply and installation of 10.9km HDPE pipes and fittings, construction of a 50m³ elevated (10m high) service reservoir, a 50m³ collection chamber, and 6 water points. It also includes construction of anchor blocks, gully crossings, and manhole points to ensure a complete water distribution system. The project concludes with full commissioning, testing, temporary handover, and final handover upon successful completion.
