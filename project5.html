CONSTRUCTION OF CATTLE REPRODUCTION AND DISTRIBUTION CENTER PROJECT

The project includes construction of 2.35km internal reinforced concrete roads, 1.8km surface drainage lines, a 75m³ collection chamber, and a 100m³ elevated water tanker, along with a 4km water distribution system and sewerage lines. It also involves construction of a 20m x 10m x 6.5m store, 4 silos, 4 elevated guardhouses, and car parking facilities. The scope further includes supply and installation of electromechanical systems and a complete underground electrical installation system.
